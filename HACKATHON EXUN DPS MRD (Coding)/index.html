About Us

Aqua Armor is a company focused on advancing underwater exploration through cutting-edge technologies. Their products include a Water Pressure-Resistant Suit for deep-sea safety, a Marine Life Tracking System for real-time monitoring of ocean creatures, and Tidal Lens AR Goggles that provide augmented reality simulations of marine life. These innovations enable explorers to safely navigate, study, and experience the ocean's depths.

Recent Sightings

    Our Solution: The TIDAL SURGE

    The Tidal Surge is a high-performance deep-sea suit crafted from nano-reinforced titanium, combining pressure resistance, flexibility, and comfort. It features ultra-bright LEDs for visibility, ultrasonic emitters for creature safety, hydro-thrusters for navigation, real-time sonar mapping, and oxygen recycling for extended, comfortable deep-sea exploration.





    Our Solution: The TIDAL LENS

    Tidal Lens is an augmented reality (AR) goggle system that simulates marine life by overlaying realistic 3D models of ocean creatures and ecosystems onto the user's environment. Designed for education, research, and recreation, it allows users to explore marine life in real time without needing to dive, offering an interactive and immersive underwater experience.
